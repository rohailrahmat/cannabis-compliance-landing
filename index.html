Frequently Asked Questions

Are your documents legally compliant?

Yes, all documents are generated based on current state regulations. However, we recommend having a cannabis attorney review for your specific situation. We provide document preparation services, not legal advice.

Which states do you cover?

We currently serve Colorado, California, Oregon, Washington, Michigan, Illinois, and Massachusetts. Contact us if you're in another state.

What if I need revisions?

All packages include at least one revision round. We'll work with you to ensure the documents meet your needs.

How fast can you deliver?

Standard delivery is 72 hours. Premium package includes 48-hour rush delivery. In some cases, we can deliver overnight.

What format will I receive?

All documents are delivered as professional PDFs with your dispensary branding, plus an editable Google Docs version for future updates.
